# XlTimeUnit enumeration (Office)

Indicates units of time measurement.

|Name|Value|Description|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
|**xlDays**|0|Specifies Day units.|
|**xlMonths**|1|Specifies Month units.|
|**xlYears**|2|Specifies Year units.|
